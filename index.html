Coderclass


I’m a young student at Metis Montessori Lyceum, located in Amsterdam, Netherlands. I follow the program course provided by Metis, called “Coderclass”. The course consists of five hours of lesson in a week, every week. Every month or so we learn another language or so called “Module”. Every Module you complete you get a Badge (As seen below). The badge is proof that you master that Module. There are some Modules that you need to do but if you finish these modules you can choose which Module you want to master next. The lessons are given in Text-Documents and sometimes in person.
